The Graphic & Web Designing course has been designed to prepare individuals to convey messages with visual communication and presentation. As part of this course, students are trained to use a combination of typography, visual arts and page layout techniques to produce both print & digital designs. Not just that, the course imparts knowledge to apply the principles and elements of design in branding and advertising. The Graphic & Web Page Designing course in a certificate course that encompasses the required training to design and develop mobile responsive websites for corporate, institutes, E-commerce site etc. The course pedagogy is a combination of practical assignments balanced with theory classes and professional seminar
